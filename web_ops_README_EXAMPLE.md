*   **X (Twitter):** Rewards high engagement velocity (replies, retweets); use punchy hooks and thread complex ideas to spark discussion.
*   **YouTube:** Values audience retention and click-through rates; ensure strong thumbnails and clear chapter markers for long-form, and rapid pacing for Shorts.
*   **Universal Drivers:** Across all platforms, the first 3 seconds are critical for retention. High-value, actionable content drives saves and shares, which are key indicators of quality to algorithms.
